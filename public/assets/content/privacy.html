Privacy Policy

At Calmiqs , we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you entrust to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information. It applies to information collected via our website, services, communications, and any other interactions you have with Calmiqs.

1. Information we collect

We collect information that helps us deliver and improve our services. The categories of information we collect include:

  • Personal information you provide: name, email address, business or organization name, contact messages you submit, newsletter sign-up data, and other details you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage and analytics data: pages visited, time on page, click behavior, referral source, events, and other usage metrics collected through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Technical information: IP address, browser and device type, operating system, language preferences, and similar technical information automatically recorded by servers and analytics systems.
  • Public information: content you publish on the site (for example, comments or public profile information) and information from third-party sources if you connect accounts or allow integration.

2. How we use your information

We use collected data for specific, limited purposes that help operate and improve Calmiqs:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our website, products, and services.
  • To respond to questions, requests, and support inquiries.
  • To send administrative information such as security alerts and account notices, when applicable.
  • To personalize content and recommend relevant information, tools, or posts.
  • To analyze and monitor usage, detect trends and security issues, and generate aggregated insights for product improvements.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions.

3. Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to provide basic functionality and to collect analytics data. Cookies may be:

  • Essential cookies: required for site functionality (e.g., session management).
  • Performance/analytics cookies: used to measure and analyze how our site is used so we can improve its performance and content.
  • Functional cookies: to remember user preferences (e.g., language or display choices).

You may control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may reduce functionality or prevent some features from operating correctly.

4. Analytics and third-party services

We use third-party providers to support our infrastructure and analytics. Typical providers include content delivery networks (CDNs), analytics services, email delivery platforms, and code hosting services. Examples include Cloudflare (CDN, DNS, security) and GitHub (code hosting / CI). These providers may receive limited technical information necessary to operate the service.

When we use analytics services (for example Cloudflare Insights or Google Analytics), data is typically aggregated and anonymized. We configure analytics to minimize personal data retention where possible and to respect users’ privacy preferences.

5. Affiliate relationships and sponsored content

Calmiqs may participate in affiliate programs and may display affiliate links to products or services we recommend. If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

Our affiliate relationships do not influence editorial independence. All recommendations are based on our assessment of value, quality, and fit for our audience. Affiliate links are disclosed clearly when present.

6. Data sharing and disclosure

Calmiqs does not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share information in limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who perform functions on our behalf (hosting, analytics, email delivery), under contractual obligations to protect privacy and limit use.
  • When required by law, court order, or to respond to lawful requests by public authorities.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Calmiqs, our users, or others — including fraud prevention and security.

7. Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including to satisfy legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Retention periods vary by data category and the nature of the service provided.

8. Security measures

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information, including TLS/HTTPS encryption for data in transit and secure storage practices for data at rest.

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the unlikely event of a security incident affecting personal data, we will follow applicable notification obligations and remediate the issue promptly.

9. International transfers

Calmiqs is a globally accessible website. Data may be processed in the country where our servers, third-party vendors, or service providers operate. By using the Site, you acknowledge and consent to the transfer of your data across international borders in accordance with this policy.

10. Children’s privacy

The Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have inadvertently gathered information from a child under the applicable age limit, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as reasonably possible.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below. We will require identity verification before fulfilling significant data requests to protect privacy and security.
