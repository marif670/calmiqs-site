What are Mind Tools?

Mind tools are simple, repeatable techniques (often digital or behavioral) that help regulate breathing, focus attention, and reduce acute stress — without medication or therapy. They include breathing exercises, timers, visualizers, and short interactive games that lower physiological arousal.
